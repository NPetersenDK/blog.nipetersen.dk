Who am I?

In my work life I work as a systemsadministrator for Aarhus University - my daily work is based around VMware solutions and systems.

I have experience in:

  • PowerShell
  • VMware: vSphere ESX and vCenter and solutions like: VSAN, NSX, Horizon
  • Windows Server: AD (+LDAP), Fileservers (+DFS), WDS and more.
  • Linux: Mostly a Debian based user, working with all kinds of Linux based services.
  • Monitoring: Zabbix (Hereunder their agent + SNMP and more)
  • Networking in general (Heavy VyOS/EdgeMAX user)

Homelab:

I have at a very young age been looking into servers and have been a homelabber for a very long time - initially it started out as just needing a fileserver, and then it took off. Going into VMware clusters, Linux and Windows servers and services like AD/SAMBA/DFS/LDAP and more.
